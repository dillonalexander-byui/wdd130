I am starting to learn blender in order to get into modding games as well. One of my favorite books that I have read is called the Toaster project written by Thomas Thwaites. The Toaster Project by Thomas Thwaites is a fascinating endeavor that highlights the complex global supply chains involved in creating seemingly mundane products. Thwaites set out to build a toaster entirely from scratch, using pre-industrial tools and methods. His journey involved collecting raw materials, smelting iron ore, and even distilling crude plastic from oil. The resulting “half-baked, handmade pastiche” of a toaster contrasts sharply with the inexpensive ones we buy today.
