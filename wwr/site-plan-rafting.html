Overview

Purpose

We created this website to expand our reach to more than our local community to further spread our joy of river rafting.

Audience

In our river rafting adventures, we want to make sure everyone is able to enjoy the trip. These are some of the goals we have in mind:

  • Who do we invite?: Everyone, whether that's family, friends, or co-workers.
  • Why do we ask you to come on these?: These trips are exciting and help to build co-operation.
  • How do we expect to get the word out?: By this website of course!
