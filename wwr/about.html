As our company name goes, We want to make "shore" you'll have fun rafting down the many rivers nature has to offer!

History

We started off as a small company based out of Colorado Springs, family-owned, serving mostly our community members and neighbors. Over the next several years, as people shared their experiences of our high-quality rafting trips,

the amount of people we had coming to try out our small family business grew quite a bit. Nowadays, people from all over the country come to try out our rafting experience, both for the excitement it brings and to see the views Colorado has to offer.
